Dead Man in his Grave | Kuany Dut | Poetry


By Kuany Dut

5 April 2026




I don’t know why But for my sake
You really longing
To die But for my sake
My darling brother.


But for my sake
Please, don’t die.But for my sake
Is it because you are
Tire of life,


That is why you
want to die?
please don’t die.


I don’t know why
You really longing
To die
My darling brother.


But for my sake
Please, don’t die.
Is it because you are
Tire of life,


That is why you
want to die?
please don’t die.
